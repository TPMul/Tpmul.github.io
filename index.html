A typographic timeline that highlights the buildings in Jamaica Plain area that used to house all of the old breweries.

Stride Gum Ad Campaign

screen

China Infographic

Group project done with Ryan Tindall that looks at China's progress since it's adoption of capitalism.

Folding Paper Inception Interface

An interactive interface designed to look at the disruptions in the planned world in the movie Inception. It follows the team through all of the levels of the dream, using a blueprint like approach to mimic the architecture that went into making the worlds we are showing.

Employ.me

Animation that covers the basic points of a service which we created that helps homeless youth get the skills and experience they need to get a job and become self sustaining.

Magic: The Database App

An interface designed to look at the disruptions to the planned out world in the movie Inception and follows the team through all of the levels of the dream.

Omni Network

Part of a brand built for the fictional tv network Omni, project included both screen materials, such as these types of intros, as well as print materials such as stationary, posters and dvd cases.
