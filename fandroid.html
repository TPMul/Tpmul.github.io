fAndroid Magazine

A monthly magazine dedicated to covering the latest news of the mobile operating system android. Now that android has moved beyond just phones and is now in wearables, game systems and more, fAndroid is your source of android news.

Tech magazines on the whole are notoriously associated with bad design, because most assume that as long as the content is good readers won’t care. This approach may work for the programmers and computer scientists, however that bothered me since I enjoy tech too, so I set out to fill that gap this magazine.
The color scheme was derived from the colors found in the operating system, with the addition of an accent orange to give it an extra pop. Each feature article is given it’s own typeface to set it apart from the rest of the magazine. The design of this magazine relies on a diagonal corner element that is used to reflect the dynamic and ever changing world of Android. The magazine also breaks down the different sections by color, stories in blue, news in green and ask in orange. Our magazine brings readers the stories but breaks down complex questions with diagrams and graphs to help them easily digest what is going on.
