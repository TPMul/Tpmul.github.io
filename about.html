about

Hi, my name is Tucker Mulcahy and I'm a senior graphic design student studying at Massachusetts College of Art and Design. Striving to create simple, clean and impactful design to effectively communicate through print and web. I have a profound interest in design and technology and love being able to combine the two. I've also had a love of photography since middle school so when I am not behind a computer screen you might find me out and about shooting.
